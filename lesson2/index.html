Purpose

This site is meant to be a place where individuals in the local area can visit to get up to date infromation on weather. On top of that, there will be news and promotional material about the towns and surround areas. The hope is that by encapsulating these pages in one location, we can generate good traffic, making this site the dependable resource for community news.
