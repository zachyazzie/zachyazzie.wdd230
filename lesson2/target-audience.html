Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this project is the everyone either visiting or living in Southern Alberta, Canada. Anyone looking for information in preparation for community events, outdoor activities, or just wanting general information.

Two Personas

Persona 1:

Name: Garrett Bobby Furgeson
Age: 30
Occupation: Electronics Technician
Demographic: Single dad. Father of one. Middle class.
Goals for using site: Uses the site to look for favorable weather for community events and outings with his child.
Technology: Personal computer and mobile device

Persona 2:

Name: Margaret Smith
Age: 27
Occupation: Reporter
Demographic: Unmarried. Middle class.
Goals for using site: Uses the site to coordinate favorable weather that corresponds times to go out in the community to look for stories.
Technology: Laptop, tablet and mobile device

5 Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Tyler and Josh are checking for the clearest day of the week for them to hold and outdoor concert in their neighborhood. They also see if there are any conflicting events in the area.

Scenario 2:

Midoriya is coordinating an outdoor event for his classmates. He needs to make sure that the weather is favorable so that there aren't any obstacles while they perform their outdoor exercises.

Scenario 3:

An older man heard that there is a large white fish in the nearby lake. He heard that it only comes out when it's raining. He needs to check the website to make sure that the park he needs to visit is open and that it's raining.

Scenario 4:

A party of nine are going on a long backpacking trip to deliver and important package and are needing to know the safest route. They check the site for any tips for the route that they're taking.

Scenario 5:

Travellers from out of the area are looking for infromation on what to do in the surrounding area. They check the site and are able to plan out their trip with local events.
