Cake Day

Soda Springs Elementary celebrated its 100th birthday by constructing a 100ft birthday cake. Of course, this wasn't entirely feasible, so as compromise, 100 1ft cakes were baked and stacked the best they could. While initially a success, the local wildlife took it upon themselves to not leave perfectly good 15ft tall cakes to go to waste. WIthin the hour there were around 50 different critters nibbling on the cake. This only ruined 2 out of the 7 cakes. So we ended up with a 70ft cake when it was all over with. Overall a resounding success!
