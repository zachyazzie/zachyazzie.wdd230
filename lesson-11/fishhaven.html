A Close Encounter

Fish Haven recently held the largest extra terrestrial convention in the state of Idaho. With almost 500 individuals in attendance, alien enthusiasts from all over shared a bond over a love of all things other worldly. Of course, there weren't any aliens in attendance that we know of. This event mostly consisted of speakers explaining UFO phenomena in the surrounding area, trying to build upon and verify local legends. The event organizers reached out to alien enthusiast Tom Delonge for a musical act, but he was tied up with prior commitments. The music was provided by local band, Mouse Rat. Their songs consist of pro-alien jams such as, "Look at that little green man" and "Aliens Live Under the Earth". The whole event was a great bonding moment for the town.
