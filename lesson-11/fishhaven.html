A Day to Celebrate

Last week, the town of Preston celebrated international waffle day by turning the day into an amazing festival. Preston is known for its deep love of breakfast foods. From the humble pancake to the complex breakfast sandwich. Being that it was international waffle day, Preston celebrated in style with a waffle cook-off! Types of featured waffles were: belgian, classic store mix, potato, and even Eggo toaster waffles. It was an all day event with music and games in the park for the family. The gates opened at 6AM and closed at 7PM. This all day festival was a true town bonding event. For all who were unable to attend, mark your calendar for next year so we can all come together as a community and celebrate the best food for breakfast!
