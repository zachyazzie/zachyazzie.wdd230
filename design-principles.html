Visual Hierarchy

DEV

Visual hierarchy is achieved on this page by placing the most important details larger and bolder. For isntance, the first suggested article has an image with it, conveying to the end use that this one is the most important. Also, the headings of each article are large and bold. Finally, the "Create account" button is colored differently, making it stand out more, so the user's eye is drawn to it more.

Repetition

Apple Music

Repetition is used on this page by repeating the square or rectangle shaped album covers. This motif is used throughout the entire site. The simplicity of the design helps the user not get confused on where or what to look at. It also gives the feeling of looking through a personal collection of music albums. I think Apple felt that theme necessary to make it feel like a music-first platform.

White Space

Wordle

White space is used on this site by having a very minimalist interface. The end user is on this page for one thing only, which is to play this game. There is no guessing how to navigate the page or what it's for. It also uses JavaScript to fill in that white space and the game proceeds, changing the feeling of the page. It's a really clever use of the principle.
